A Publisher That Puts Authors First

At Lakefront Publishing Group, we believe the relationship between a publisher and an author should be built on trust, transparency, and shared purpose. We are a small, independent press. That means every author we take on receives our full attention from the very first conversation to the final printed page.

We are selective by design. We would rather do a few things exceptionally well than stretch ourselves thin across dozens of projects. If we take on your book, it means we believe in it, and we will work tirelessly to make sure it reaches the readers it deserves.

What We Publish

We are currently focused on two areas where we can offer the deepest expertise and the most meaningful impact:

Nonfiction

Thought-provoking titles in business, decision-making, leadership, and ideas that challenge the way we think and work.

Children's Books

Warm, beautifully illustrated books for toddlers and young readers. Especially titles that introduce children to careers and the people who inspire them.

Emerging Voices

We are open to compelling manuscripts that don't fit neatly into a category. If your idea is strong, we want to hear it.

Not Sure?

If you have a manuscript and you're unsure whether it's a fit, reach out anyway. We're always happy to have a conversation.

Our Process

How It Works

1

Submit Your Query

Send us a brief overview of your manuscript: your concept, your audience, and why you believe it matters. No full manuscript required at this stage.

2

Initial Review

We review every submission personally. If your project is a potential fit, we'll reach out to learn more and request additional materials.

3

Editorial Development

We work closely with you to refine and develop your manuscript, from structural editing through to final copyediting and proofreading.

4

Design & Publication

Our team handles cover design, interior layout, printing, and distribution so your book reaches readers looking and feeling its very best.
