Decision Intelligence: Why Evidence Fails and How Leaders Win the Room

By Nicholas Johnson

Coming September 2026

Every day, good decisions die in rooms full of smart people with good data. The problem isn't the evidence — it's how it's presented, framed, and received. Decision Intelligence gives leaders a practical framework to understand why evidence so often fails to persuade, and what it actually takes to make it land.

Whether you're a business leader, a data professional, or anyone who has ever watched a great idea get ignored, this book will change the way you think about decisions, influence, and the gap between knowing and doing.
