Who We Are

Lakefront Publishing Group is an independent, full-service publishing company founded on the belief that powerful ideas deserve to be shared with the world. We are dedicated to working with authors to produce books that are thoughtfully written, beautifully designed, and built to stand the test of time.

From nonfiction titles that challenge the way readers think, to children's books that spark curiosity from the very first pages, our catalog is intentionally diverse and carefully curated. We take pride in the quality and integrity of every title we publish.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to bring meaningful stories and ideas to life. We believe that every great book begins with a great idea, and our role is to provide the expertise, care, and craftsmanship to help that idea reach its fullest potential.

As an independent publisher, we are not driven by volume. We are driven by impact. We work closely with a select group of authors, giving each project the individual attention it deserves from manuscript to finished book.
